# Title Worksheet: Viral Book Title Generator

**Use this worksheet to brainstorm Gen Z-optimized book titles that stop scrolling and drive clicks.**

---

## Step 1: Define Your Book's Core

### Book Concept
**In one sentence, what's your book about?**
```
Example: "Teaching Gen Z entrepreneurs how to start profitable side hustles in 90 days"
```

**What's the ONE big promise?**
```
Example: "Make your first $10K from a side hustle"
```

**What transformation happens?**
```
From: _________________ (current state)
To: ___________________ (desired outcome)

Example:
From: Stuck in 9-5 with no extra income
To: Running a profitable side business making $10K+
```

## Step 6: A/B Test Plan

**How to validate your titles before committing:**

### Social Media Test
Post all 3 titles as:
- Instagram story polls (which would you read?)
- Twitter/X posts (track engagement)
- Reddit posts in target subreddits (check comments)

### Search Intent Test
Google each title + "book" and see:
- Are similar titles ranking?
- What's the search volume trend?
- Any gaps you can fill?

### Friend Test
Send to 5 people in your target age range and ask:
1. "Which title makes you want to click?"
2. "What do you think the book is about?"
3. "Would you actually buy this?"

**Record results:**
```
Title 1 Score: ___ / 15 (social + search + friends)
Title 2 Score: ___ / 15
Title 3 Score: ___ / 15
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Too generic:** "How to Start a Business"
✅ **Specific:** "The $10K Side Hustle Playbook"

❌ **Too corporate:** "Strategic Approaches to Entrepreneurship"
✅ **Conversational:** "Side Hustle Speedrun: Broke to Boss"

❌ **Boring promise:** "Learn About Money Management"
✅ **Exciting promise:** "Why Your Parents' Money Advice is Broke"

❌ **No timeframe:** "Build a Profitable Business"
✅ **Timeframe included:** "First Sale in 48 Hours"

❌ **Vague benefit:** "Improve Your Life"
✅ **Concrete benefit:** "Make Your First $10K"

---

## Quick Checklist

Before finalizing your title, check:
- [ ] Is it under 80 characters? (fits on book cover + social posts)
- [ ] Does it include a number or timeframe? (specificity builds trust)
- [ ] Would you click this on Instagram? (scroll-stopping test)
- [ ] Is there a clear benefit/outcome? (what do I get?)
- [ ] Does it sound like a human wrote it? (not AI-generated corporate speak)
- [ ] Would your target audience use these words? (language match)
- [ ] Is it searchable? (includes keywords people actually Google)
- [ ] Does it avoid clickbait? (delivers on promise, not misleading)
