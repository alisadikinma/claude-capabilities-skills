## Retrospective Format

**Format Used:** [Select one below or specify custom]

### Option 1: Start-Stop-Continue
- **START:** What should we start doing?
- **STOP:** What should we stop doing?
- **CONTINUE:** What's working well that we should keep doing?

### Option 2: Mad-Sad-Glad
- **MAD:** What frustrated us or made us angry?
- **SAD:** What disappointed us or made us sad?
- **GLAD:** What made us happy or proud?

### Option 3: 4Ls (Liked, Learned, Lacked, Longed For)
- **LIKED:** What went well this sprint?
- **LEARNED:** What did we learn or discover?
- **LACKED:** What was missing or could be improved?
- **LONGED FOR:** What do we wish we had?

### Option 4: Sailboat Retrospective
- **Wind (Helping):** What propelled us forward?
- **Anchors (Hindering):** What slowed us down?
- **Rocks (Risks):** What risks lie ahead?
- **Island (Goal):** What's our destination?

- Engine (accelerators): What sped us up?

- Keep Doing
- Less Of
- More Of

- Start Doing
