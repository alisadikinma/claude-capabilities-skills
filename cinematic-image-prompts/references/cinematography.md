# Cinematography Reference

Quick lookup tables for lighting, lens, film stocks, and emotion mapping.

---

## Emotion → Expression → Body Language

| Emotion | Facial Expression | Body Language |
|---------|------------------|---------------|
| **Shock** | Wide eyes, raised brows, open mouth | Frozen, pulled back |
| **Intrigue** | Narrowed eyes, head tilt, focused gaze | Subtle forward lean |
| **Curiosity** | Bright eyes, raised brows, engaged | Leaning in, open posture |
| **Tension** | Furrowed brow, tight lips, intense gaze | Rigid shoulders, clenched |
| **Awe** | Softened eyes, open mouth, relaxed brow | Chin up, shoulders relaxed |
| **Confidence** | Steady gaze, subtle smile, relaxed brow | Squared shoulders, steady |
| **Authority** | Unwavering gaze, knowing smile | Arms crossed or steepled |
| **Contemplation** | Downcast eyes, neutral mouth | Hand near chin, head tilt |
| **Excitement** | Bright eyes, genuine smile, lifted brows | Animated, energetic |
| **Concern** | Furrowed brow, tight lips, worried eyes | Pulled back, defensive |
| **Warmth** | Soft eyes, genuine smile, relaxed | Open posture, slight lean |

## Lighting Patterns

| Pattern | Key Position | Shadow | Mood | Prompt Phrase |
|---------|-------------|--------|------|---------------|
| **Rembrandt** | 45° side, above | Triangle on cheek | Dramatic, authority | "Rembrandt lighting, triangle shadow" |
| **Butterfly** | Above, on-axis | Under nose | Glamorous, beauty | "butterfly lighting, paramount" |
| **Split** | 90° side | Half face | Intense, duality | "split lighting, half-face shadow" |
| **Loop** | 30-45° | Small nose loop | Natural, flattering | "soft loop lighting" |
| **Rim/Edge** | Behind | Glowing outline | Separation, drama | "strong rim light, edge separation" |
| **Broad** | Lit toward camera | Shadow away | Wider appearance | "broad lighting" |
| **Short** | Shadow toward camera | Shadow forward | Slimming, moody | "short lighting, sculptural" |

## Lighting Ratios

| Ratio | Contrast | Use Case |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **2:1** | Low, subtle | Beauty, commercial, explainer |
| **4:1** | Moderate | Standard drama, dialogue |
| **8:1** | High | Thriller, noir, tension |
| **16:1** | Extreme | Horror, extreme drama |

## Aperture & Depth of Field

| Aperture | DOF | Application |
|----------|-----|-------------|
| f/1.4-1.8 | Extremely shallow | Subject isolation, heavy bokeh |
| f/2.8 | Shallow | Portraits, cinematic |
| f/4 | Moderate | General (Deakins preference) |
| f/5.6-8 | Deep | Groups, environment visible |
| f/11-16 | Very deep | Landscapes, all sharp |

## Camera Angles

| Angle | Setup | Psychology |
|-------|-------|------------|
| **Eye-level** | At subject eye height | Neutral, equal |
| **Low angle** | Below eye, up | Powerful, heroic |
| **High angle** | Above, down | Vulnerable, weak |
| **Dutch** | 15-45° roll tilt | Unease, tension |
| **Bird's eye** | Directly overhead | Omniscient |
| **Worm's eye** | Ground level up | Maximum power |
